Table Top Games

Warhammer

In the Grimdark future, there is only war! Warhammer has been a longstanding wargame that is played at the Hall, and only continues to grow. Tournaments are held often, and almost there is almost always someone willing to throw down some dice in this amazing tabletop wargame!

X-Wing

Star Wars Mini's? Check. Epic Space battles? Check. Played at the Hall? Check. X-wing is another trademark game at the Hall, and attracts players of all ages that wage battles of all sizes. Get together your ships and crew and start your thrusters!

Dungeons and Dragons

One of the, if not the most, iconic tabletop games of all time, Dungeons and Dragons is a pen and paper RPG that is limited to only your imagination. Epic quests, huge monsters, and forging friendships within your gaming group await you in this classic tabletop game!

Card Games at the Hall

Magic the Gathering

Trading card games are among some of the most fun you can have in a gaming store because it caters to everyone! Whether you're a Power gamer that wants the strongest deck, a collector that wants rares, or a casual looking for some competetive fun, no game captures this like Magic the Gathering. Having amassed a huge following in the Hall, opponents are in no shortage, and teachers can be found. The staff are knowledgeable about this game because they are all players themselves! Competitions are held often, and there is something for every type of gamer

Board Games

Wall O' Games

The Hall features a shelf that is dedicated to community board games. These are all open to the public, and allows you to try one of the many games before commiting and buying it. Whether looking for a few fun rounds, or looking to demo, feel free to try these games out!

Board Games for Sale

If you tried out a game from our Wall, or if you read about a fun board game online, you can always buy it from us so you can take the fun home! We have a variety of games, and if we don't have it, we can surely order it!
